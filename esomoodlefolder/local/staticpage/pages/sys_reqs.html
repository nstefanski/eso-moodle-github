System Requirements

Internet Connection: Cable or DSL
Operating System: XP, Vista, Windows 7, Mac OSX or greater
Computer Processor: CPU with SSE2 support, including Intel Pentium 4, Pentium M, Core or Atom, AMD Athlon 64 or later
Computer Memory: 512 MB RAM or greater
Screen Resolution: 1024 x 768 minimum
